# Five-Minute Explanation Rubric

| Area | Points | Requirement |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Bug | 25 | Names the actual broken behavior |
| Evidence | 20 | Uses thread dump or simulated evidence |
| Mechanism | 25 | Explains why the failure happens |
| Fix | 15 | Shows the code-level fix |
| Cost | 15 | Explains performance/design tradeoff |

Keep the explanation technical and concise. Do not narrate every line of code.
